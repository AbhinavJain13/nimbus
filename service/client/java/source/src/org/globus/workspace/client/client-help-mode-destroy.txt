
Definition:
==========

Destroys a single workspace, a group of workspaces, or an ensemble.  If a group
or ensemble EPR is given, the service will destroy each workspace in the group
or ensemble that has not already been destroyed.


Required for destroy:
====================

--eprFile (-e) <file>  EPR of the single workspace, group workspace, or ensemble
                       resource to destroy.

Also see "--help" and "--extrahelp"
